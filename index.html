Brief Biography 

Mr. Yi-Heng Zhu received the BS degree in computer science and technology from Nanjing Institute of Technology in 2015. From 2015 to 2023, he was a Ph.D student with the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology and a member of Pattern Recognition and Bioinformatics Group, led by Professor Dong-Jun Yu. From 2019 to 2021, he acted as a visiting student at the University of Michigan (Ann Arbor) in U.S.A, funded by China Scholarship Council and led by Professor Yang Zhang. He is currently a teacher with the College of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing Agricultural University, and a member of Center for Data Science and Intelligent Computing, led by Professor Zhiwei Ji. He is interested in the following topics:
